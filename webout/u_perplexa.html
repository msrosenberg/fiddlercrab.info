Type Description

Gelasimus perplexus
Milne Edwards, H. (1852) Observations sur les affinités zoologiques et la classification naturelle des crustacés. Annales des Sciences Naturelles. Zoologie (Série 3) 18:109–166.

Information

Taxonomy
Subfamily Gelasiminae → Supertribe Gelasimitae → Tribe Gelasimini → Genus Austruca → Subgenus Austruca
Common Names
Perplexing Fiddler Crab
Synonyms, Alternate Spellings, & Name Forms (Chronology)
Austruca orientalis, Austruca perplexa, Gelasimus annulipes var. orientalis, Gelasimus annulipes var. perplexa, Gelasimus annulipes var. perplexus, Gelasimus perplexus, Uca annulipes subsp. orientalis, Uca annulipes var. orientalis, Uca lactea perplexa, Uca perplesca, Uca perplexa
Size
Small Carapace Breadth: 8.1 mm ± 3.09 (sd), 95% range: 2.1–14.2 mm, (Data)
Geographic Range
Indo-West Pacific: found throughout virtually the entirety of the central and western Pacific islands, between American Samoa to the east and the Andaman Islands along the eastern edge of the Indian Ocean to the west, including continental Malaysia, eastern Thailand, Cambodia, and possibly Vietnam; Taiwan; the Ryukyu Islands; eastern Australia
