Information

Taxonomy
Subfamily Gelasiminae → Supertribe Gelasimitae → Tribe Gelasimini → Genus Austruca → Subgenus Sinduca
Common Names
English: Indus Fiddler Crab
Synonyms, Alternate Spellings, & Name Forms (Chronology)
