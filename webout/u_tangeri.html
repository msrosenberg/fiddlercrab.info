Type Description

Gelasimus tangeri
Eydoux, F. (1835) Nouvelle espèce de Gélasime. Magasin de Zoologie 5(7):29–32.

Information

Taxonomy
Subfamily Ucinae → Supertribe Ucitae → Tribe Ucini → Genus Afruca
Common Names
West African Fiddler Crab; European Fiddler Crab; Barrilete
Synonyms, Alternate Spellings, & Name Forms (Chronology)
Afruca tangeri, Gelasimus atlanticus, Gelasimus cimatodus, Gelasimus perlatus, Gelasimus Tangeri, Gelasimus Tangerii, Gelasimus tangieri, Uca Afruca tangeri, Uca tangari, Uca tangeri, Uca tangeri var. matandensis, Uca tangerii, Uca tangieri
Size
Large Carapace Breadth: 14.9 mm ± 8.80 (sd), 95% range: 0.0–32.1 mm, (Data)
Geographic Range
Eastern Atlantic: West coast of Africa from Angola to Morocco, São Tomé and Príncipe, Cape Verde, Canary Islands, southwestern Spain and southern Portugal
