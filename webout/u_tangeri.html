Subgenus
Afruca
Common Names
West African Fiddler Crab; European Fiddler Crab; Barrilete
Synonyms, Alternate Spellings, & Name Forms
Gelasimus atlanticus, Gelasimus cimatodus, Gelasimus perlatus, Gelasimus Tangeri, Gelasimus Tangerii, Gelasimus tangieri, Uca (Afruca) tangeri, Uca (Gelasimus) Tangeri, Uca (Minuca) tangeri, Uca (Uca) tangeri, Uca tangari, Uca tangeri, Uca tangeri var. matandensis, Uca tangieri
