Information

Subgenus
Leptuca
Common Names
Crenulated Fiddler Crab, Notched Fiddler Crab, Mexican Fiddler Crab
Synonyms, Alternate Spellings, & Name Forms
Gelasimus crenulatus, Gelasimus gracilis, Uca (Celuca) [crenulata] crenulata crenulata, Uca (Leptuca) crenulata, Uca (Minuca) crenulata, Uca crenulata, Uca crenulata crenulata, Uca gracilis
Geographic Range
Eastern Pacific: USA (rarely as far north as Santa Barbara) to Mexico (Nayarit)
