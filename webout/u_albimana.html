Type Description

Gelasimus annulipes var. albimana
Kossmann, R. (1877) Zoologische Ergebnisse einer Reise in die Küstengegenden des Roten Meeres III. Crustacea. Leipzig: Wilhelm Engelmann.

Information

Taxonomy
Subfamily Gelasiminae → Supertribe Gelasimitae → Tribe Gelasimini → Genus Austruca → Subgenus Austruca
Common Names
English: White-handed Fiddler Crab
Synonyms, Alternate Spellings, & Name Forms (Chronology)
Austruca albimana, Gelasimus annulipes var. albimana, Uca albimaba, Uca albimana, Uca annulipes albimana, Uca lactea albimana
Size
Medium Carapace Breadth: 12.9 mm ± 5.48 (sd), 95% range: 2.1–23.6 mm, (Data)
Geographic Range
Indo-West Pacific Realm: northern Somalia to eastern United Arab Emirates, including Socotra, Gulf of Aden, Red Sea, southern Gulf of Oman, and southeastern Persian Gulf
