Information

Taxonomy
Subfamily Gelasiminae → Supertribe Gelasimitae → Tribe Minucini → Genus Minuca
Common Names
Pacific Hairback Fiddler Crab; Ecuadorian Fiddler Crab
Synonyms, Alternate Spellings, & Name Forms (Chronology)
Minuca ecuadoriensis, Minuca lanigera, Uca ecuadoriensis, Uca ecuatoriensis, Uca lanigera, Uca schmitti, Uca vocator ecuadoriensis
Size
Medium Carapace Breadth: 19.4 mm ± 4.40 (sd), 95% range: 10.8–28.0 mm, (Data)
Geographic Range
Eastern Pacific: Mexico to northern Peru
