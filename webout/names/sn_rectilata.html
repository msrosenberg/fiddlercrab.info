Fiddler Crabs

rectilata

  • Full Name Index

History

Primary Senior Synonym
Unknown
Original Usage
Gelasimus rectilatus
Original Source with Priority
Lockington, W.N. (1877) Remarks on the Crustacea of the west coast of North America, with a catalogue of the species in the museum of the California Academy of Sciences. Proceedings of the California Academy of Sciences 7(1):145-156.
Derivation
Named for the much more rectangular carapace shape than other species.

Notes/Comments

Type specimens were destroyed in a fire and the descriptions and figures are inadequate to clearly place with a specific species. The name has been discarded.

Binomials Using this Specific Name

  • Uca (Minuca) rectilata
  • Uca rectilata
  • Uca rectilatus
