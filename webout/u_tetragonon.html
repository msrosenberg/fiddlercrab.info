Information

Taxonomy
Subfamily Gelasiminae → Supertribe Gelasimitae → Tribe Gelasimini → Genus Gelasimus → Subgenus Mesuca
Common Names
Tetragonal Fiddler Crab
Synonyms, Alternate Spellings, & Name Forms (Chronology)
Cancer tetragonon, Cancer tetragonus, Gelasimus affinis, Gelasimus desjardinii, Gelasimus Duperreyi, Gelasimus tetragonon, Gelasimus tetragonum, Gelasimus variatus, Ocypoda tetragona, Ocypode tetragona, Uca affinis, Uca duperryi, Uca tetragnon, Uca tetragona, Uca tetragonon, Uca tetragonum
Size
Medium Carapace Breadth: 16.2 mm ± 5.97 (sd), 95% range: 4.5–27.9 mm, (Data)
Geographic Range
Indo-West Pacific: present on most islands from the east coast of Africa to the central Pacific Ocean; rarer continental populations observed Kenya, the Red Sea, the mouth of the Persian Gulf, western Malaysia and Thailand, and the east coast of Australia
