Postage Stamps

Fiddler crabs have been featured on postage stamps surprisingly often. Quality control leaves something to be desired, however, as misidentifications are common (e.g., see The Gambia and the Solomon Islands). Omori & Holthuis (2000, 2005) have actually written papers about crustacea on postage stamps.
