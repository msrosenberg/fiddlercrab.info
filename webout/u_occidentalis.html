Size
Medium Carapace Breadth: 15.7 mm ± 3.09 (sd), 95% range: 9.6–21.7 mm, (Data)
Geographic Range
Indo-West Pacific: east coast of Africa from South Africa to Somalia, Madagascar, Réunion, Mauritius, Mayotte, Seychelles
